For Businesses

When you run your own business, you wear many hats. Cook, crafter, accountant, CEO. "Internet expert" shouldn't be one of them. That's where Vendabond comes in - we help the little guy get the word out on what they have to offer and where to get it.

Get a web and mobile presence to let your customers know what's on the menu. Fresh out of bacon-waffle-bourbon cheeseburgers? Update your menu in real time. Traveling on the road? Set up shop and instantly tell your customers and fans where you are. With Vendabond, your website and social media is quickly updated from the same smartphone or tablet you use to accept credit cards.

Vendabond is a whole suite of tools designed to make you business easier to run, and more accessible to your customers. Use as many or as few as makes sense for you. Every chef's art is different, with different needs to suit their goals. That's why we offer menu display, real-time location tracking, daily deals, and more.
